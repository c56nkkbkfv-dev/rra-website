Why we rise
“They told us it was safe. But our elders knew something was wrong. The land changed. The food changed. Our lives changed.”

The story of Rongelap is not just a timeline. It is family. It is homeland. It is elders carrying memories that should have been protected, not ignored.

Rongelap Rising Alliance exists so this story is told clearly, respectfully, and with the power it deserves. We rise for truth, for healing, and for the generations still waiting for justice.
